Master Jewelry Designer & CAD Artisan

With over 20 years of experience in custom jewelry design and development, I specialize in creating bespoke engagement rings, wedding bands, and luxury jewelry pieces. My journey began in the traditional goldsmithing workshops of Colombo, Sri Lanka, where I learned the sacred art of metalworking from master craftsmen.

Today, I combine this rich heritage with cutting-edge CAD design technologies, creating timeless pieces that honor tradition while embracing innovation. From diamond rings to custom necklaces, each piece tells a unique story of precision craftsmanship and artistic excellence.

My work has been recognized internationally with prestigious awards including the 2018 Presidential Gold Award and 2017 Jewellery This USA Award.
