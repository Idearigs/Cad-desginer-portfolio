Master Jewellery & Watch Designer

Since 1998, I have been devoted to the art of custom jewelry design, beginning my journey in the traditional goldsmithing workshops of Colombo, Sri Lanka. Trained by master craftsmen, I learned the timeless values of precision, patience, and artistry.

Today, I combine this heritage with modern CAD technology to create bespoke engagement rings, wedding bands, and luxury jewelry pieces. Each design is crafted to tell a unique story, blending tradition with innovation.

My work has earned international recognition and prestigious awards, a testament to my commitment to excellence. With over two decades of experience, I continue to craft timeless jewelry that celebrates love, heritage, and individuality.
