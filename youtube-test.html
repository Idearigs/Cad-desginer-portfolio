Common Issues:

  • Embedding Disabled: Video owner disabled embedding
  • Privacy Settings: Video is private/unlisted with restrictions
  • Region Restrictions: Video blocked in your country
  • Age Restrictions: Video requires age verification

Solutions:

  • Ask video owner to enable embedding
  • Use a different video that allows embedding
  • Convert to MP4 and host locally (if you have permission)
  • Use the youtube-nocookie.com domain (Test 3 above)
